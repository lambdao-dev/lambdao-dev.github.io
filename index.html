Our mission:
  • Putting you in control of your data
  • Making the (computer) tools work for you

In other words, our mission is Quality, Transparency, Durability. A required condition is for the tools to be malleable. Cost should be a consequence of these, because of ease of change and maintenance.
Our approach:
  • Test Driven Design
  • Git: rigorous version control
  • Open Source: GPL licensing
  • Weathered technologies: robustness, safety, longevity
  • Open Standards, no vendor lock-ins
  • Data ownership: keep it private
